# Vocabulary of Kikuyu

## Greetings
- Hello: _Nĩwĩ._  
- How are you?: _Wĩ mwega?_

## Numbers
- One: _Ĩmwe_  
- Two: _Iĩrĩ_

## Food Items
- Water: _Maji_  
- Bread: _Gĩcungwa_
